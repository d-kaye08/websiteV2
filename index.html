Industry Experience
This page will briefly cover all my current and previous employment opportunities (as of September 2023) within the geospatial science industry. The two main companies that I have worked at were SmartX and MNG. This page will outline the experience and skills I've gained in the industry along with an overview of my current technological skills.

SmartX

GIS Officer & Surveyor
April 2022 to July 2023
My time at SmartX was my first role in the surveying and geospatial industry, which provided me with invaluable experience. In my role, I utilised the Esri Suite (ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, Esri Field Apps) and Esri's coding language (Arcade), FME (Feature Manipulation Engine), Trimble Business Centre, as well as AutoCAD.

Tasks from the geospatial aspect involved developing and building an asset management system for a client. This involved utilising ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online and Esri Field Maps. This system enabled the client to collect asset information in the relevant SPEC data format in the field. Linked dashboards in the ArcGIS Online environment then enabled the client to interact and produce reports from their data logged in the field. This system not only provided a way for the client to log new assets, but also enabled corresponding maintenance information to be recorded to each feature. Thorough planning delivered more than the client was expecting, but also allowed the potential for the client to onsell their data in the future.

Tasks from the surveying aspect involved processing aerial imagery and cleaning point clouds to produce deliverables for clients. These deliverables were contour maps, earthwork reports and as-builts.

MNG

GIS Analyst
June 2023 - Present (as of September 2023)
I had the opportunity of working with MNG through their 2023 MNG Scholarship. This role improved my technical knowledge of the GIS software (QGIS) and gave me experience within GlobalMapper.

Tasks involved updating Google's roads dataset by using aerial imagery, where roads in new developments were digitised. Updated spatial data in the company's internal system, by identifying static data and finding the equivalent APIs, whilst respecting the license requirements. I also georeferenced survey plans for clients to view in the company system. QGIS templates were made (profile and drafting templates) for company wide deployment.
